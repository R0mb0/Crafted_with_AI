## Story of this repository

"I previously created a "[Not Made By AI](https://github.com/R0mb0/Not_made_by_AI)" badge. But after working with AI, I realized that focusing strictly on 'Human-made' in the professional world is anachronistic, as many tasks would no longer be economically sustainable without it. That's why I decided to create a new badge to celebrate the collaboration between humans and AI.

### Why should I include this badge in my work?
  
  </summary>  

This badge is a form of self-declaration that you used AI to assist in your work. While some people omit this fact to appear more skilled than they actually are, displaying this badge is a sign of intellectual honesty. It shows humility, acknowledging that you don't overestimate your abilities and that you might not fully understand every single line of the generated code.
